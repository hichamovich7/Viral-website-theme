Celebrities Who Have Aged Gracefully and Flawlessly

- Tanya Roberts


ads 2

Though she hasn’t been seen in anything since 2005, we will always remember Tanya Roberts being nominated for worst actress when she portrayed a Bond Girl in A View to a Kill. Before her days as Midge on That ’70s Show, Roberts was also a Charlie’s Angels replacement, which then led to a starring role in the 1982 blockbuster The Beastmaster. She garnered quite a bit of attention for posing in Playboy to promote the film. Her career aside, there is no denying that she looks great for her age at 62.
